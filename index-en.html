I want to know the services!

What we do

Queremos que su público de interés tenga acceso a productos amigables, bien estructurados y con información relevante. Buscamos la mejor forma de posicionar su mensaje, tomando en cuenta las particularidades de cada contexto.

Subject development on Latin American issues

We identify academic and governmental sources, relevant topics, and speakers to help you to develop, boost and carry out your academic programmes.

Research projects in Latin America

We offer a flexible back-up package to help you to accomplish your research tasks. Depending on the type of project, this includes:

  • Training before travelling to Latin America
  • Agenda setting (fixer service)
  • Data collection
  • Research interview transcription
  • Translation
  • Press

Contacts between universities

We identify suitable institutions and develop one-to-one links to increase your visibility and promote your interests among local universities.

Alianza Chile-Australia

  • Ofrecemos la posibilidad de impulsar contactos que conduzcan a acuerdos bilaterales entre universidades.
  • Identificamos, contactamos y gestionamos la visita de expertos australianos a Chile.
  • En sesiones personalizadas, lo asesoramos sobre qué esperar y cómo desenvolverse en el contexto cultural australiano.

Nosotros

Somos periodistas, hemos estudiado nuestros posgrados en Australia y hemos trabajado por visibilizar Chile en Australia y Australia en Chile a través de la investigación y contactos bilaterales en la academia, gobierno y organizaciones no gubernamentales.

Francisca Díaz de Valdés

Francisca’s extensive experience in communications and strong focus on international relations are key factors in identifying how to solve needs within the academic and industry spheres. As a journalist, she has successfully managed the coordination of events and the dissemination of corporate content in both Chile and Australia where she graduated from The University of Melbourne as a Master of International Relations.

Since graduating from The University of Melbourne she has used her qualifications and experience to create collaborative research projects between Australia and Chile.

Irene Strodthoff

Irene’s diverse background and experience within multiple areas of the communication industry and academic sector have shaped her unique approach to Conexión Sur.

Irene has worked in the areas of communication and publishing, and received postgraduate degrees in International Communication from Macquarie University and Latin American Studies from The University of Sydney.

She has managed a number of complex and high profile research and communication projects over the course of her professional career. In the last 15 years she has been actively engaged in publishing research and in boosting the visibility and close relations of Chile and Australia.
